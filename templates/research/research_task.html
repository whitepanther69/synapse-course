🎯 Your Mission

You are a security tester. ShopSecure has hired you to investigate 3 vulnerabilities reported by a previous audit. Your job: reproduce the attack, document the impact on CIA (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability), and recommend a mitigation.

🔍 The 3 vulnerabilities to find:

1. Insecure Deserialisation (CWE-502) — The app imports user preferences using Python pickle. Can you exploit this to execute commands on the server or escalate your privileges?
2. Path Traversal (CWE-22) — The app serves invoice files. Can you read confidential server files outside the intended directory?
3. CSRF (CWE-352) — The transfer form has no protection. Can you build a page that steals money from a logged-in user without their knowledge?
